Quantum Tunneling — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Quantum Tunneling (the “App”) is a single-player arcade game. We take a simple approach to privacy: the App does not collect any data about you.

Data we collect

None. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts and no sign-in.

Network and tracking

The App runs entirely offline using locally generated data. It does not require or use a network connection to play, contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking or data-collection SDKs.

On-device data

Any preferences (such as display settings) are stored only on your device by the operating system and are never sent anywhere.

Children

Because the App collects no data, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If a future version adds features that use the network or collect data, this policy will be updated before that version is released.
